BE A MAN: RESPECT WOMEN

People on the Internet, television, and places such as school and the workplace can be discourteous to each other. For example, if you watch videos online, there is always a troll in the comments section being extremely rude to others or not expressing their opinion in a well-mannered way. The way these trolls behave is disrespectful and they cause disharmony in the online community in which they are participating. I feel it is important to respect people, even people you might not particularly like. Not being respectful can show that you have poor character, are closed-minded.
